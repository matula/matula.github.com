How this works

The gist

Predict the 2026 FIFA World Cup. Pick scores for every match, lock in your Champion, and call who lifts the bronze. As real results come in, your picks score automatically.

The three tabs

Groups — predict the final score of every group-stage match. Type a number into each side's box; it saves the instant you change it.

Bracket — pick your Champion and 3rd place right now from the dropdowns at the top. Knockout matches show as TBD until teams advance, then you can predict scores there too.

My Picks — a scored list of every prediction you've made. Filter by stage, export to JSON, import a backup, share your bracket as a URL, or reset everything.

Your running total is the pill in the top-right. It stays — until the first match finishes.

Sharing

The Share URL button on My Picks encodes your full bracket into a link. Anyone who opens it gets prompted to import your picks.

Storage

Picks live in localStorage on this browser only — there's no account. Clearing site data wipes them, so use Export if you want a backup.
